
North Star Metric

Can a cancer patient, regardless of their stage or situation, understand their next best step within 3 minutes of entering the platform?

目标：通过内容 + AI Search 友好结构，成为癌症患者的流量入口。

验收标准：

AI 是否容易理解并引用？
用户进入后是否进入 Journey？
Question 是否是高意图决策入口？
是否形成 Cancer ↔ Decision Moment ↔ Question ↔ Treatment ↔ Story 的关系？


建议的「入口成立」标准（可验收）
比「收录了多少页」更准的是：

患者用自然语言问一个决策问题 → AI/搜索能否 点名或引用你们的结构（答案块 + 边界）
点击进入后 → 3 分钟内是否进入 What to do next（北极星）
是否继续走到 Journey 下一节点（不只 bounce）
三者同时成立，才叫「癌症患者流量入口」；只满足第 1 条只是「被引用的内容站」。


「验证一个问题能否成为入口资产」比「有多少问题」更重要——这应成为下一阶段唯一北极星（流量侧）。

Global Cancer Decision Platform
Technical Product Specification v1.0
AI-Powered Cancer Decision Knowledge Platform

Version: 1.0
Target: MVP Development
Platform: Web Application
Primary Language: English

1. Product Overview
Product Name

Global Cancer Decision Platform

Product Positioning

This product is NOT:

Cancer encyclopedia
Medical diagnosis system
Online hospital marketplace
Doctor replacement

This product IS:

An AI-powered cancer decision knowledge platform helping patients understand diagnosis, compare treatment options, explore global medical choices, and learn from real patient decision journeys.

2. Core Product Concept

The core asset is:

Cancer Decision Knowledge Graph

The website is organized around:

Cancer Type

      ↓

Patient Situation

      ↓

Decision Question

      ↓

Treatment Options

      ↓

Global Options

      ↓

Patient Experience

      ↓

Next Decision

The system should NOT be built as a traditional blog.

It should be built as a structured knowledge database.

3. MVP Objectives

The first version should prove:

Objective 1

AI Search discoverability.

The website should expose hundreds of structured decision pages.

Objective 2

Build reusable cancer knowledge architecture.

Adding new cancer types should require data entry, not new development.

Objective 3

Create trust through:

structured answers
references
patient decision stories
transparent limitations

17. Development Principles

Important:

Principle 1

Build data architecture first.

Do not hard-code pages.

Principle 2

Every cancer should use the same template.

Principle 3

Content scale comes from database + AI generation.

Principle 4

The core product is:

Knowledge Graph.

Not articles.

18. Definition of MVP Success

MVP is successful when:

5 cancer categories exist
500 structured questions can be stored
100+ pages can be published
Internal linking works
Search engines can understand relationships
Adding new cancer requires no code change
Final Product Definition

Build an AI-native cancer decision knowledge platform that transforms fragmented cancer information into structured patient decision pathways.
